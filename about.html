WHO ARE WE?

Devdaha English Boarding High School located at the Devdaha Muncipality ward no. 7 Pipaldanda is a secondary school running classes from playgroup to 10 .Established in 2053 Bs DEBHS has achieved many remarkable milestones in Academic as well as Extra Curricular Sector .Our mission is to produce ideal citizens for the nation .We are improving day by day , step by step to create a environment that fosters the holistic development of our lovely students and allow their talent to shine in society.
